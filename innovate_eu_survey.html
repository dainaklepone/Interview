About This Survey

The INNOVATE-EU project, funded by the European Union's Horizon Europe Framework Program for Research and Innovation 2021-2027, aims to overcome the innovation divide between European regions and strengthen European start-ups through interconnected and inclusive innovation ecosystems.


This survey focuses on building connections between different European regions, specifically Aragón (Spain), Baden-Württemberg (Germany), Latvia, Lithuania, and Northeast Romania. The estimated time for completion is approximately 45 minutes.
